Worker Safety

Lost Value

About the Project

A Student-Led Initiative for Environmental Innovation

WasteIQ was developed as part of a university startup competition with the goal of addressing one of the world's fastest-growing environmental challenges. As a student passionate about both technology and sustainability, I recognized the opportunity to apply cutting-edge AI to make a real-world impact.

This project combines computer vision, deep learning, and environmental engineering to create a practical solution that recycling facilities can actually use. By leveraging the proven AlexNet architecture, WasteIQ offers an accessible, affordable approach to intelligent e-waste management.

The vision is simple: transform how we handle electronic waste by making AI-powered classification available to facilities worldwide‚Äîimproving both worker safety and environmental outcomes.
